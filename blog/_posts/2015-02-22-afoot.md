One of the most important things for a developer to do is to start blogging.

I can't count the number of times I have been told this  directly or have read
it on some blog somewhere. The most recent time was in [John Sonmez's book Soft
Skills.](http://www.manning.com/sonmez/). John has been extremely helpful as of
late and therefore I thought that I should take him up on the challenge of
starting this blog.

The core of the problem is what to write about. I have had the same arguments
that I think most of us have when we hear this advice. What possibly could I
blog about that has not already been blogged about? Surely everyone knows about
what I am about to blog, why should I spend the time? What if everyone finds out
I am an imposter?

The answers are always the same. Just blog. Someone, somewhere will find it
useful. There is always someone starting out that does not know it.

I finally believe it or at least I have convinced myself to take the leap of
faith and just blog anyways.
