Today I am writing a little off the beaten path. Today I resigned my position as a developer at J.D. Heiskell & Co.

I have been battling with the need for something more from my career and unfortunately I hit a plateau with my current job.
I hit the limit of what I could continue to learn from my team and the ability to stay motivated to continue to learn.
This all boiled down to the fact that I was stuck learning things an not being able to apply what I learned to my everyday
development practices.

The second issue I came into was that I was having a hard time increasing my value to the company. Whether that was becasue I
did not understand what I needed to do or the company was not able to help me define it well enough it kind of up in the air.
I think we both tried, but we were coming at it from different angles and experiences and I completely understand.

Change is something that I am very fearful of as I have 3 kids and a wife who depend on me and my work, so I resigned without
having a solid plan in place or knowing where I was going to end up. I had the support of my wife and kids and they allowed me
to resign without that plan.

So here we are... At a fork in the road... I am going to take a few weeks off to travel and see some friends, meditate and
see what comes my way.

I know that I have several opportunities in the works, but it just is a matter of where I fit in now.
